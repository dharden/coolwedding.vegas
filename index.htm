Travel / Accomodation

Fly into McCarran International Airport (LAS).

The best way to travel from the airport is probably Uber or Lyft. If you've never used Uber or Lyft before, they are ride-hailing apps that you can download for your Android phone or iPhone. Use promo code "6micy" for an Uber discount and "DAHA67" for a Lyft discount!

You can also take a shuttle, but they tend to take forever. If you're from out of town, taxis will try to long-haul you, so watch out. Don't let them take the highway, it's a trap!

We recommend staying at the Tropicana Hotel and Casino. We have a room block available! You can book rooms directly from this link.

If you'd like to stay somewhere else, the closest hotels to the Tropicana are the Excalibur, MGM Grand, and New York New York. Also, Hooters Hotel & Casino is next door and usually VERY CHEAP - stay at your own risk. Anywhere else will be a further walk than you think.

Check out hotelscombined.com for the best rates.
